OUR PRODUCTS AND SERVICES
LDPE Granules
Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is a thermoplastic chemically formulated from monomer ethylene LDPE has extensive usage in manufacturing ACP sheet, various containers, wash bottles, tubing, plastic bags for computer components and other molded laboratory equipments Our range of LDPE Granules known for: (1) Durability and Accurate composition
HDPE Granules
High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) Granules, a new product launched primarily used for making Water Tank and is known for their large strength to density ratio and are extensively used for making water & milk jar, containers, drummer, HDPE pipe, irrigation pipe, telecom industry, edible oil container, medicine jar & boxes, plastic bottles, corrosion resistant piping, geomembrane and plastic lumber. Our range of HDPE Granules known for (1) Accurate composition (2) Effectiveness
PP Granules
Polypropylene (PP) Granules, a form of thermoplastic polymer is used for making house hold items & toys, chair pallet crate in applications as packaging, labeling, textiles, stationery and reusable containers of various types, etc. Our range of PP Granules known for:- (1) High color quality (2) Purity
